The four week framework

How The Money Reset works

Each week builds on the last. By week four you will have a complete money system running in your life.

01
Track

Go through one full month of spending and get complete clarity on your income, expenses and savings. Most people are genuinely surprised by what they find.

02
Audit

Identify your money leaks, cut what is not serving you, compare your bills for better deals, and close the gap between where you are and where you want to be.

03
System

Set up your bucket system — every dollar that comes in has a place to go. Bills. Spending. Emergency savings. Goal savings. Set up once, runs automatically.

04
Consistency

Build your weekly money date habit — a 10 to 15 minute check in that keeps you on top of your finances and on track towards your goals long term.

Everything you need to know

Is this program right for me? +
If you earn money but feel disorganised, inconsistent or out of control with it — yes. This program is designed specifically for women who want to get the foundations right before moving into investing and wealth building.
How much time does it take? +
The program is designed to be worked through one week at a time over 30 days. Each lesson is short and easy to consume. The tracking exercise in week one requires the most dedicated time — set aside a couple of hours for that one. Everything else fits around your life.
Do I need to earn a certain amount? +
No. This program works at any income level. The skills you will learn are about managing what you have — not about earning more. That said it is designed for women who are already earning and want to make their money work better for them.
What if I fall behind? +
This is self paced so there is no falling behind. Work through it at a pace that suits you. You have lifetime access so you can come back to it whenever you need a reset.
Is this financial advice? +
No. The Money Reset is a financial education program and not personalised financial advice. For advice specific to your individual circumstances please consult a licensed financial adviser.
